French A1 Media
News Sources

20 Minutes Fr

20 Minutes Fr is a free daily newspaper with concise, easy-to-read articles. It covers general news, politics, culture and sports in a straightforward and accessible style.

Characteristics: Simple sentence structure, common vocabulary, short summarized articles

Skills Improved: Speaking, Grammar, Cultural Awareness



News in Slow French

News in Slow French provides global news articles, podcasts and op-eds in simple, easy language meant for beginners! Here, cognates will help you build your French vocabulary!

Characteristics: Simple sentence structure, common vocabulary, short summarized articles

Skills Improved: Speaking, Grammar, Cultural Awareness



Le français facile avec rfi

RFI's le français facile provides simplified articles of world news and podcasts, and is specifically designed for French beginners! Tip: For any simplified news source, just search for the "news source" + en facile!

Characteristics: Simple sentence structure, cognates, short summarized articles

Skills Improved: Listening, Reading Cultural Awareness
